**Effective: April 21, 2026**

The refund policy of the naholo service (the "Service") operated by Mano Technology Co., Ltd. (the "Company") is governed as follows in accordance with the Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce.

## Article 1 (Free Trial Period)

1. New users are provided with a 1-month free trial.
2. No fees are charged during the free trial period, and no costs are incurred if the User cancels before the trial ends.

## Article 2 (Conditions for Refund)

Refunds are available in the following cases:

1. **Service failure**: when the User cannot use the Service normally due to a reason attributable to the Company
2. **Duplicate payment**: when duplicate payments occur for the same service

## Article 3 (Refund Procedure)

1. Users may request a refund through customer support (fluke8259@gmail.com).
2. Users who no longer have account access (for example, those removed from a project) may also request a refund directly through the <a href={portalUrl}>Polar customer portal</a> without signing in to naholo.
3. The Company will notify the User of the refund decision within 3 business days from the date the refund request is received.
4. When a refund is approved, it will be processed within 7 business days.

## Article 4 (Refund Deadline)

Refunds due to service failure shall be processed within 30 days from the date the failure is confirmed.

## Article 5 (Refund Method)

1. Refunds are processed through the Polar payment platform to the original payment method.
2. The refund amount is calculated on a pro-rated basis for the unused portion of the usage period.
3. No payment is incurred during the free trial period; therefore, no separate refund is required when cancelling during the trial.

## Article 6 (Dispute Resolution)

1. In the event of a dispute regarding a refund, the Company and the User shall sincerely consult to resolve it.
2. If no agreement is reached, matters shall be governed by the Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce and related laws.
